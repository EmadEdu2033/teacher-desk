Teacher Desk for Windows
========================

Thank you for downloading Teacher Desk!

How to install
--------------
1. Double-click  TeacherDeskSetup.exe
2. If Windows shows a SmartScreen warning ("Windows protected your PC"),
   click  More info  ->  Run anyway.
   (This is normal for new apps that are not yet code-signed.
    Teacher Desk only stores data on your own computer.)
3. Click  Yes  on the Windows User Account Control prompt.
4. The setup wizard will open. Click  Next, accept the install
   folder (or pick your own), then click  Install.
5. When it finishes, click  Finish. Teacher Desk will start
   and you will get shortcuts on your Desktop and Start Menu.

How to start Teacher Desk every day
-----------------------------------
- Double-click the  Teacher Desk  icon on your Desktop, OR
- Press the Windows key, type  Teacher Desk, and press Enter.

What you get
------------
- A wall of sticky notes you can drag, resize, color, and resize fonts on.
- A task list with priorities, due dates, sub-tasks, and reminders.
- English and Arabic with right-to-left layout.
- Light and dark themes.
- Podium mode: hide Teacher Desk from Zoom / Google Meet / OBS while
  you can still see it.
- Backup and restore — your data lives only on this computer.

Where your data is stored
-------------------------
   %APPDATA%\Teacher Desk\teacher-desk.db

(Press Win+R, paste the line above, and press Enter to open the folder.)
You can also open this folder from inside the app:
   Settings -> Open data folder.

How to uninstall
----------------
Windows Settings  ->  Apps  ->  Teacher Desk  ->  Uninstall.
Your notes and tasks stay in %APPDATA%\Teacher Desk so you can
reinstall later without losing anything. To remove your data too,
delete that folder by hand after uninstalling.

Need help?
----------
- The app works fully offline; no account, no internet required.
- If something looks wrong on first launch, close Teacher Desk,
  then reopen it once. The local database initializes on first run.
